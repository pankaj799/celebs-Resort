About Us

If you are looking for a calm and celestial  peace along with Your “Me time” then Celeb's Resorts is the place for you. Situated in Shoghi hills, this tourist destination is at the height of 5700 ft. An off beat destination gives you 360 degree view of forest, hills and valley. A place where cold breeze would make you forget all your worries and make you closer to yourself. Amidst nature bounty, special bamboo house could  provide the much needed tranquility and seclusion.  Shoghi is just 100 kilometers from Chandigarh and 370 kilometers from Delhi on the national highway. Travel time from Chandigarh is three hours by taxi. There is 24 hours bus services from Delhi and Chandigarh a number of Volvo buses also ply daily on this route.
