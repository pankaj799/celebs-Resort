About Us

Indian charm comes to life at Celebs Restaurants & Bars, our stunning Himachal's resort, immersed between blues skies and the Mountains. Set amongst the stunning natural geography of the Shimla alongside the Mountains, Celebs Restaurants & Clubs affords extraordinary views of the Himachal Pradesh in the entire region.

OUR HOSPITALITY

Cultivated with care, our chef’s herb garden, surrounded by hibiscus, bougainvillea and oleander, is a truly special choice for your event. Choose to add a fascinating culinary experience to your meal, beginning with a tour of the garden. An interactive experience, you will learn how to incorporate indigenous ingredients into your own kitchen and leave with a deeper appreciation for Indian cuisine. Our dining team can transform your favourite Mountain Front spot into an exclusive dinner venue. With an inimitable menu highlighting our exceptional local produce, and décor and flowers that suit your style, we will make your dining experience a truly special occasion.
